Fine Art

Adults

Fine Art lessons by LAPA cater to students from beginners all the way to be aspiring artists. The modules provided would cover skill training on drawing, painting, murals, and mix media productions. Fine Arts cover both the theoretical and practical approaches in its teachings, and they are both in-studio and online.
